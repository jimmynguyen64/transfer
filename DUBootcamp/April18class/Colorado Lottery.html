Best Friday the 13th Ever for Commerce City Man

Apr 13, 2018

Surprised at work ... on FRIDAY THE 13TH!

The Lottery Prize Surprise crew caught our 2nd Chance winner for Mega Hit Crossword Multiplier at work at the American Legion LCW Post 1 in Denver. 
 
Jimmy N. from Aurora won $250,000 with a non-winning Scratch ticket, as his friends, co-workers, and customers looked on and celebrated with his. She called her husband to break the news and he said he can't wait to pay off their house.
 
Jimmy said a close friend from the Legion recently passed away and his children immediately came to mind when she realized she'd won big.
 
Congratulations, Jimmy and family!
